Blockquotes

Nothing special. Just neat looking blocks of quotes.

Here’s to the crazy ones. The rebels. The troublemakers. The ones who see things differently. While some may see them as the crazy ones, we see genius. Because the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world, are the ones who do.


<blockquote>

<p>Here's to the crazy ones…</p>

</blockquote>

We provide, of course, a method to add author information to quotes:

Here’s to the crazy ones. The rebels. The troublemakers. The ones who see things differently. While some may see them as the crazy ones, we see genius. Because the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world, are the ones who do.

—Apple's Think Different Campaign 

<blockquote>

<p>Here’s to the crazy ones... </p>

<small>—Apple's Think Different Campaign</small>

</blockquote>

If you want your blocquote on the right then use <blockquote class="right"> … </blockquote>
